How it works
  1. Connect your wallet on BNB Chain (MetaMask, etc.).
  2. Approve the staking contract for CMNK (first time only).
  3. Enter the CMNK amount and press Stake.
  4. Use Claim Rewards anytime to receive earned CMNK.
  5. Use Unstake to withdraw your staked CMNK (no lock time).
